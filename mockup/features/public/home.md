# Home Page

**Route:** `/`

**Role:** Public

**Description:** The public landing page that presents the Denti-Code brand and directs users to the login page. It displays three role-based portal cards (Admin, Doctor, Patient) with descriptions and icons, plus a language switcher in the top right corner.

**Key Elements:**
- Hero section with title, subtitle, and "Login" CTA button
- Three feature cards for Admin, Doctor, and Patient portals
- Language switcher component
- Footer

**File:** `src/app/page.tsx`
